# Hackathon Strategy

## Why LaunchLens is a fast-fit UCWS project

The UCWS scoring model rewards public clarity, implementation quality, and expert-level product value. LaunchLens is designed around those exact signals.

## Differentiation

Many hackathon entries focus on agents, plugins, and AI workflows. LaunchLens focuses on a shared bottleneck: every team needs a stronger submission package before community voting, AI evaluation, and expert judging. That makes the product useful to the event ecosystem itself.

## Evaluation Alignment

### Community Vote

- Immediate browser demo.
- Clear first-screen utility.
- Useful to other participants, not only judges.
- Copy/export makes the product shareable.

### AI Evaluation

- Simple repo structure.
- No hidden backend dependency.
- README, deployment docs, tests, and screenshots included.
- Scoring logic is inspectable in JavaScript.

### Expert Judges

- Solves a real deadline problem for builders.
- Global applicability beyond UCWS.
- Clear expansion path: authenticated Project Wall import, repo-health checks, multi-event templates, and team collaboration.

## Recommended final pitch angle

LaunchLens is not another generic writing assistant. It is a submission-readiness product for hackathon teams, tuned to the workflow and evaluation reality of UCWS.
